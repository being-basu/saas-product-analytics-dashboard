# Project Overview

The SaaS Product Analytics Dashboard is a full-stack analytics platform for understanding customer growth and product engagement across the SaaS lifecycle. It brings together user, event, subscription, and feature-usage data to help teams evaluate acquisition, onboarding, monetization, and retention.

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** React.js
- **Backend:** Node.js and Express.js
- **Database:** SQLite
- **Analytics:** SQL queries over relational product and subscription data

## Business Metrics

The dashboard tracks user acquisition and signups by channel and country, onboarding activity, subscription plans and revenue, customer retention, product-event activity, and feature adoption and usage. These metrics help product and growth teams identify valuable acquisition sources, understand engagement, and monitor recurring-business performance.
